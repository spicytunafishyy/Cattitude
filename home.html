What is Cattitude?

Feeling hopeless about driving? Unsure how you're gonna handle the road? With Cattitude, we can turn that around!

Cattitude is a cat-themed, general driver's ed tool meant to aide you in your driver's ed journey. After all, driving is all about feline good! We offer randomly-generated multiple choice mini-assessments, helping you internalize general driving rules and safety tips so that you can travel safely as you take the wheel.
